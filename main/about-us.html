About Us

I am Piyush Jain,

co-founder of Nowara Realty, driven by a vision to transform Farukhnagar into a world-class city. Born in Farukhnagar and raised in Gurgaon, I was inspired by my father’s success as a real estate developer. This upbringing instilled in me a passion for the industry and a desire to give back to my roots.
As a chartered accountant, I gained extensive experience working with some of the top builders in the country. This expertise equipped me with the skills and insights necessary to embark on this new venture. At Nowara Realty, I am committed to delivering innovative commercial and residential projects that are both affordable and aspirational, aiming to enhance the quality of life for our community.

"I believe in the power of real estate to create lasting positive change, and I am dedicated to making a meaningful impact in Farukhnagar."



I am Manoj Kumar,

co-founder of Nowara Realty, and I have deep roots in Farukh Nagar, where I was born and raised. Throughout my life, I have earned the respect of my community through my integrity and unwavering commitment to my work.
As I reflect on the potential of Farukh Nagar, I believe it’s time for our city to reach new global heights. My vision for Nowara Realty goes beyond profit; it is about making a lasting impact. Together with my dedicated team, I aspire to reshape the map and image of Farukh Nagar, creating spaces that enhance the lives of our residents and reflect our community's spirit.

"At Nowara Realty, we are driven by a passion for transformation and a commitment to excellence. Join us as we embark on this journey to elevate Farukh Nagar and make a difference."
